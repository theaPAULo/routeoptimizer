Getting Started

Creating an Account

  1. Download DriveLess from the App Store
  2. Sign in with Apple ID or Google account
  3. Grant location permissions for best experience
  4. Start planning optimized routes!

Basic Route Planning

  1. Enter Start Location: Type address or tap "Use Current Location"
  2. Add Stops: Add up to 8 intermediate stops
  3. Enter Destination: Type your final destination
  4. Optimize: Tap "Optimize Route" for best order
  5. Navigate: Export to Google Maps or Apple Maps

Features

Route Optimization

  • Smart Ordering: Automatically arranges stops for shortest route
  • Traffic Awareness: Considers real-time traffic conditions
  • Round Trip: Option to return to starting location
  • Multiple Stops: Plan routes with up to 8 stops

Saved Data

  • Route History: Automatically saves completed routes
  • Favorite Routes: Save frequently used routes with custom names
  • Saved Addresses: Store home, work, and custom locations
  • Quick Access: Tap saved address chips for instant input

Daily Usage

  • Free Tier: 25 route optimizations per day
  • Reset: Usage counter resets daily at midnight
  • History: View all past routes regardless of daily limit

Troubleshooting

Location Issues

Problem: "Use Current Location" not working

Solution:

  1. Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  2. Find DriveLess and set to "While Using App"
  3. Restart the app

Route Problems

Problem: Route calculation fails

Solutions:

  • Check internet connection
  • Verify all addresses are valid
  • Try fewer stops if hitting limits
  • Ensure locations are accessible by car

Sign-In Issues

Problem: Cannot sign in with Google/Apple

Solutions:

  • Check internet connection
  • Update to latest iOS version
  • Try signing out and back in to your Apple/Google account
  • Restart the app

App Performance

Problem: App running slowly

Solutions:

  • Close other apps to free memory
  • Restart your device
  • Update to latest app version
  • Clear route history if very large

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is DriveLess free?

A: Yes! 25 route optimizations per day are completely free.

Q: Does DriveLess work offline?

A: No, route optimization requires internet connection for Google Maps data.

Q: Can I use DriveLess for commercial delivery routes?

A: Yes! DriveLess is perfect for delivery drivers, sales routes, and business travel.

Q: How accurate are the time estimates?

A: Times include real-time traffic data from Google Maps for maximum accuracy.

Q: Can I customize the route order manually?

A: Currently, DriveLess automatically optimizes for shortest time/distance. Manual reordering may be added in future updates.
