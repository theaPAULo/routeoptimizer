Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2025

Introduction

DriveLess ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our route optimization mobile application and web service.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • Current location: Used for route optimization and "use current location" features
  • Route data: Start, end, and waypoint locations you enter
  • Purpose: To calculate optimal routes and provide navigation assistance
  • Storage: Processed in real-time, route history stored locally on your device

Account Information

  • Email address: From Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In
  • Display name: From your Google or Apple account
  • Unique user ID: For account management and data synchronization
  • Purpose: Account creation, authentication, and cross-device sync

App Usage Data

  • Route calculations: Daily count for usage limits
  • Saved routes: Your favorite and historical routes
  • Saved addresses: Home, work, and custom locations you save
  • App preferences: Settings and customization choices
  • Purpose: App functionality and personalized experience

Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps: Route calculation and mapping services
  • Google Places: Address autocomplete and place details
  • Firebase: Authentication and data storage
  • Purpose: Core app functionality

How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate optimal routes with multiple stops
  • Provide real-time traffic information
  • Save your preferences and route history
  • Authenticate your account across devices
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Route history and preferences stored on your device using Core Data
  • Cloud Storage: Account info stored securely with Firebase
  • Encryption: All data transmitted using industry-standard encryption
  • Access: Only you can access your personal route data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Google Services: Location data sent to Google Maps API for route calculation
  • Apple/Google: Authentication data handled by Sign-In services
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

  • Access: View your saved routes and account information in the app
  • Delete: Remove saved routes, addresses, and account data
  • Opt-out: Disable location services in your device settings
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to permanently delete your account

Children's Privacy

DriveLess is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
